What are the danger levels?

The danger levels are derived from machine learning algorithms that analyze a multitude of factors contributing to the likelihood of fire occurrence in different regions of Kazakhstan. The algorithm calculates a coefficient for each area, synthesizing variables such as weather conditions, and historical fire incidents.
